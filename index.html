Typing Tips & Techniques

Improve your typing skills with these proven strategies

Proper Hand Position

Keep your fingers on the home row (ASDF and JKL;). This position allows you to reach all keys with minimal movement.

Look at the Screen

Train yourself to look at the screen instead of your hands while typing. This improves speed and reduces errors.

Practice Regularly

Set aside 15-30 minutes daily for typing practice. Consistency is more important than lengthy practice sessions.

Focus on Accuracy First

Build accuracy before speed. It's easier to get faster when you're accurate than to fix bad habits later.

Develop Rhythm

Try to maintain a steady rhythm while typing, even if it means typing slower at first. Speed will come naturally.

Learn Proper Technique

Each finger is responsible for specific keys. Learning proper finger placement will significantly improve your typing.
